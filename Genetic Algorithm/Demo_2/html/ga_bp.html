IV. BP神经网络

1. 网络创建

net = newff(minmax(p),[S1,4],{'tansig','purelin'},'trainlm');

net.trainParam.show = 10;
net.trainParam.epochs = 2000;
net.trainParam.goal = 1.0e-3;

3. 网络训练

[net,tr] = train(net,p,t);

V. GA-BP神经网络

S2 = size(t,1);

aa = ones(S,1)*[-1,1];
